#vss365 One Year Anniversary

September 05, 2017 is a special day in #vss365 history. It is the one year anniversary of the writing prompt! At the time, to celebrate the milestone, prompt originator Mark A. King announced that a picture prompt would be posted every hour for 24 hours on the anniversary. Here, you will find those images for continued writing inspiration!
