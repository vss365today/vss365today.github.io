The prompt for 6/26: Raft

An enlightened lesson

Many do not know

After crossing shores by raft

Where's the raft to go?

Should rafts be left behind?

What of the oar?

What's a rafter to do?

Ask why they were designed:

Bring you to shore,

Or be clung to?
