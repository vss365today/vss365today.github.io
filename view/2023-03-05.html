Today's writing prompt is:

savor

Dictionary.com Merriam-Webster

Prompt by poetguygabe

View tweet

When you are able to,

let the heaviness of the world

slip into nothingness

and be light as a feather

when you are ready

to come back.

Let the softness

of your solitude

caress your heart

whole again,

and remember to savor

every single thing you love.
